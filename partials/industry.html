For industry

Governments, NGOs and industry are three very different playing fields on which the new innovations of electronic democratic processes are evolving. But one goal is common for every field; Creating trustworthy and secure IT. Due to the extensive experience of DemTech and the span of the expertise within the research group–from cryptography and security to social sciences and voter's trust–DemTech has a profound understanding of the many and heterogeneous challenges.


How to collaborate

Collaboration with DemTech provides an opportunity to tap into the state-of-the-art knowledge from newly researched democratic technology and experiences from previous and ongoing projects around the globe. At the same time DemTech can gather data from your experiences and use them anonymously in our research. Any confidential information will not be disclosed, naturally.
