About DemTech

Computers are gradually replacing the manual parts of the electoral process. However, software systems are complex, prone to programming error, and are vulnerable to malicious attacks. If not addressed early, these problems will have a negative effect on the very foundations of democracy.


DemTech provides answers on how to modernize the democratic process without jeopardizing the fundamental principles on which democracies are built. To do so, DemTech focuses on the voting technology as a whole, concentrating not only on technology and security, but also on questions related to organization, trust, and social choice.


DemTech brings together internationally leading experts on voting technology and elections. Our international research staff collaborates, communicates, and consults with governments, administrations, industries and academics worldwide.
